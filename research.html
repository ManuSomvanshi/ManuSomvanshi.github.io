Resources to get started on Surfaces and Mapping Class Groups

Below are some amazing resources to start learning about surfaces, hyperbolic geometry, mapping class groups, and big mapping class groups.

  1. A primer on Mapping Class Groups by Benson Farb and Dan Margalit.
  2. Big Mapping Class Groups: An overview by Javier Aramayona and Nicholas Vlamis.
  3. Geometry and Spectra of Compact Riemann Surfaces by Peter Buser.

The first and last are books accesible to an undergrad in their final year. The review on Big MCGs is accesible to anyone familiar with surfaces and mapping class groups of finite type surfaces.
